chat Example Interaction — CNA: Maria S.  |  Resident: Mr. Johnson
🚪 Entry
Maria *knocks on door* Good morning, Mr. Johnson?
Mr. Johnson Yes, come in!
Maria I'm Maria, your CNA today. I'm here to take your vital signs — I'll be checking your pulse, respirations, and blood pressure. It won't take long and should be comfortable. Is that okay with you?
Mr. Johnson Sure, go ahead.
🧴 Setup
Maria I'll just close the curtain for your privacy. *closes curtain, washes hands, gathers equipment, puts on gloves*
⚕️ Performing the Skill
Maria I'm going to place my fingers on your wrist to count your pulse — you'll just feel a light touch.
Mr. Johnson No problem.
Maria *counts pulse and respirations silently* Now I'll check your blood pressure. You'll feel the cuff squeeze your arm — let me know if it's uncomfortable.
✅ Wrap-up
Maria All done! Here's your call light in case you need anything. *removes gloves, washes hands* Are you comfortable? Is there anything else I can do for you?
Mr. Johnson I'm good, thank you Maria.
Maria You're welcome! I'll go report your results to the nurse right away. Have a great morning!
